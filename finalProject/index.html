Reflection

Throughout my journey here at Salt Lake Community College, I have never been more challenged in a class more than CSIS 1430. I have really learned to lock down key concepts in HTML and CSS and have learned all the necessary tools to set me up for success going forward. The lectures and hands on examples made my learning experience top notch, making it easy to grasp the content being taught. I’m a smarter student now coming out of this class and i really appreciate the time my professor took to make sure all the students were on the same page and moving forward with progression.

I can’t wait to continue my journey in learning the concepts of computer science. I can really see how my education in the topic will help me in the long road ahead of me by not only getting a career within the field but it will also allow me to open many new doors as i create priceless relationships through networking. I hope you enjoy going through the different projects that i have created throughout this class. You can see a variety of examples i personally designed using HTML, CSS, JavaScript and BootStrap.
